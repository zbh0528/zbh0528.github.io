Biography

I am a Ph.D. Candidate (D3) at the University of Toyama, Japan, specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Evolutionary Computation.

My research interests include Swarm Intelligence, Multi-objective Optimization, and Feature Selection. I am dedicated to developing robust algorithms to solve complex, large-scale real-world optimization problems.
